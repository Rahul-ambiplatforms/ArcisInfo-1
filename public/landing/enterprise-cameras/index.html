Why NDAA Compliance Matters for Your Organization

The US National Defense Authorization Act (Section 889) permanently bans the use of video surveillance equipment from five Chinese companies in federal agencies, their contractors, and organizations receiving federal funding.

This affects government buildings, military installations, banking institutions, healthcare facilities, educational institutions receiving federal grants, and any company doing business with the US government.

  • 🚫 Hikvision — World's largest CCTV maker, banned entity
  • 🚫 Dahua (including Lorex, Amcrest) — Banned entity
  • 🚫 Huawei / ZTE — Telecommunications banned entities
  • 🚫 Hytera — Communications equipment banned entity

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ArcisAI truly NDAA compliant? +
Yes. ArcisAI cameras contain zero components from any NDAA-banned entity (Hikvision, Dahua, Huawei, ZTE, Hytera). We provide full Bill of Materials (BOM) documentation and an official NDAA compliance letter for your procurement team upon request.
Where are ArcisAI cameras manufactured? +
ArcisAI cameras are designed and manufactured in India by Adiance Technologies. India is a TAA (Trade Agreements Act) designated country, making ArcisAI products eligible for US government procurement through GSA Schedule and other federal acquisition vehicles.
Will ArcisAI cameras work with my existing VMS? +
ArcisAI cameras support ONVIF Profile S, T, and G, making them compatible with virtually any modern VMS platform including Milestone XProtect, Genetec Security Center, Avigilon ACC, VMukti Cloud VMS, and many others. RTSP streaming is also supported.
What AI capabilities are built into the cameras? +
Every ArcisAI camera includes edge AI processing for: people detection, vehicle detection, intrusion detection, line crossing, loitering detection, face detection, and object classification. Advanced models include ANPR/LPR, PPE detection, crowd counting, and behavior analytics — all processed on-camera with no external server required.

Yes. ArcisAI offers a comprehensive OEM/ODM program for system integrators, distributors, and VARs. Custom branding (housing, packaging, GUI), custom firmware, and product certification support are available. Minimum order quantity starts at 100 units for white-label programs.
